History

There's no better way to start the day than with Aidantyfancy's Bangin' Breakfast Burrito. Literally no other way. I tried the other day and I ended up in a time loop like they do in Groundhog Day. I simply have to start my day with it. Aidantyfancy made this recipe when he was only three years old! His mother gave the nanny the day off but forgot about that, so went to work (dad abandoned when he was one). He had no other choice but to safety pin his diaper, pull up the highchair, and get to work. Unfortunately, this resulted in Aidantyfancy's fingertips being burnt off, and he's now an uncatchable jewlery thief in northern France.

I know what you're probably thinking: do people regrow fingertips? And the answer is no. But, with this incredible breakfast dish, you can start your day off with hard work that is only paralleled by Aidantyfancy's three-year-old self. It's super easy, delicious, and stuffed with breakfast!
