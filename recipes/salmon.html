Delicious Brown Sugar Salmon

History

A generational dish, the Delicious Brown Sugar Salmon has been passed through Aidantyfancy's family like no other recipe. In 1738, Lilytyclassy was out playing with the local brown bears in the South Carolina woods, when she caught her first salmon by mouth. The locals called her "Queen of the Trout" on account of them not knowing of any other fish. But Lily knew she could do more with this fish than any of her ancestors did with trout - she just didn't know what yet.

With the help of her lovely husband Jonnytyfabulous, they experiemented for 25 tiring nights, as they were both employed as Laugh Factory workers during the day, and finally settled on the phenomenal Brown Sugar Salmon recipe. Lilytyclassy recorded the recipe on a pizza box and reserved it in resin. It has been passed through Aidantyfancy's family ever since.

Ingredients

  • One large salmon, preferably caught by mouth
  • Three spoonfuls of brown sugar
  • Salt
  • Garlic power
  • One tbsp oil
